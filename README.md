## Project Overview

This dashboard was built to simulate a real-world sales analytics scenario for an automotive retail business. The goal was to take raw transactional sales data and transform it into a decision-ready dashboard that a sales or business team could actually use to track performance, spot trends, and identify opportunities.

## Key Metrics Tracked

- **YTD Total Sales:** $371.19M, with a 23.59% year-over-year growth indicator and $54.28M in MTD total sales
- **YTD Average Price:** $28.0K per vehicle, with a -0.79% YoY change and $28.26K MTD average price
- **YTD Cars Sold:** 13.3K units, up 24.57% YoY, with 1.92K MTD cars sold
- **Weekly Sales Trend:** Tracked sales fluctuations across 52 weeks, peaking at $14.9M in a single week
- **Sales by Body Style:** Broken down across SUV, Hatchback, Sedan, Passenger, and Hardtop categories
- **Sales by Color:** Distribution across Pale White, Black, and Red vehicle colors
- **Sales by Dealer Region:** Geographic map visualization highlighting performance across regions including Pasco, Janesville, Middletown, Aurora, Scottsdale, Greenville, and Austin
- **Company-wise Performance:** Ranked table comparing YTD average price, units sold, and percentage contribution to total sales across manufacturers including Chevrolet, Dodge, Ford, Volkswagen, Mercedes-Benz, Mitsubishi, and Oldsmobile

## Key Techniques Used

- **DAX Measures:** Built custom calculated measures for YTD Total Sales, YTD Average Price, YTD Cars Sold, and their corresponding MTD comparisons
- **Interactive Filters:** Implemented slicers for Body Style, Dealer Region, Transmission Type, and Engine Type, allowing users to drill into specific market segments
- **Geographic Visualization:** Used a map visual to plot dealer regions by sales volume, making regional performance instantly visible
- **Trend Analysis:** Built a weekly sales trend line chart to identify seasonal patterns and peak selling periods
- **Data Modeling:** Structured relationships between sales transactions, dealer information, and vehicle attributes for accurate cross-filtering across visuals
- **UI Design:** Designed a clean, dark-themed dashboard layout to improve readability across dense numerical data and multiple visual elements

## Data Source

Automotive sales transaction dataset including fields such as company, model, dealer region, body style, color, engine, transmission, price, and sale date.

## What I Learned

Building this dashboard taught me that the hardest part of data analysis isn't creating charts — it's deciding which metrics actually matter to the people who'll use the dashboard. I focused on designing for a sales manager's perspective: what would they need to see in 10 seconds to make a decision? That shaped every filter, KPI card, and visual placement in this project.

## Tools Used

Power BI Desktop, DAX, Power Query
